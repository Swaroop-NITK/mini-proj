The way we travel is changing. With technological advances and a desperate need for greater sustainability, vehicles are becoming cleaner and smarter. As a result, electric vehicles are becoming the future of transport. One of the biggest benefits of electric automobiles is that they produce zero emissions when they’re running. As there is no process of combustion, no fuel is burned, and no CO2 emissions are released into the atmosphere. Of course, these vehicles still need to charge the batteries. Sales of electric vehicles are showing signs of growth, this will eventually lead to increase in demand for the batteries. There will be long queues of customers at battery swapping centers waiting for long hours. That will create a very bad experience for the customers who are in the desperate need for the batteries.

BBS is a key solution to accommodate these customer preferences under a unified digital roof. BBS provides a technology platform that connects intending customers with the dealers of battery centers enabling convenience and transparency for the customers and the dealers. This application allows customers to book batteries at their convenience so that they need not to stand in long queues wasting a lot of time at centers. Also, the dealers can operate their businesses and manage the customers’ bookings easily. At BBS, we believe that all great properties deserve to be discovered. That’s why we make it quick and easy for dealers all around the world to market their properties, reach new customers and grow their business via our platform providing a good user experience to the customers.
